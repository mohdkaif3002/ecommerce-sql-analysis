# 📊 Key Findings — Olist E-Commerce Analysis

## Sales & Revenue
- Revenue grew 20x from R$46K (Oct 2016) to R$1.1M+ (2018)
- November 2017 was peak month at R$1.15M (Black Friday)
- Top 3 categories: Bed & Bath, Health & Beauty, Computers & Accessories

## Customer Behaviour
- 96.88% of customers are one-time buyers
- São Paulo has highest volume — 39,155 customers, R$5.77M revenue
- Bahia has highest revenue per customer at R$187

## Delivery & Operations
- Average actual delivery: 12.6 days vs estimated 23.7 days
- Olist delivers 11.2 days earlier than promised on average
- Worst sellers have 35%+ late delivery rates

## Advanced Analysis
- Used LAG() for month-over-month revenue growth
- Used RANK() OVER PARTITION to rank sellers by category
- Office Furniture top seller generated R$468K alone
